| v3.6.3 | 2026-05-02 | Center the trailing icons in each My Skills category card on mobile. The icon container was a `grid grid-cols-3 sm:grid-cols-4 md:grid-cols-5 place-items-center` — so a 5-icon category like DEVOPS rendered the bottom 2 icons in columns 1-2 (left-indented under the top 3) instead of centered.
