Three compounding bugs meant the entry URL's `utm_*` params were being lost: (1) **`lazyOnload` timing race** ŌĆö both the gtag library and its init script loaded `lazyOnload` (deferred until the main thread is fully idle, often seconds in), but the mount-time `trackPageView` fired earlier and hit the `if (typeof window.gtag !== "function") return;` guard in `utils/gtag.ts`, so the **landing page_view was silently dropped** ŌĆö and that first hit is the only one that carries the entry `utm_*`.
